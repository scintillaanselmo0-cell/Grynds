Chapter 01
TRADING

What it really is

Buying and selling financial assets — stocks, currencies, commodities, indices — trying to profit from price differences over time. It's not magic. It's not luck. It's a job with precise rules and a learning curve that takes years.

How the process works

  • 01Open an account with a regulated broker (Interactive Brokers, Degiro, Saxo)
  • 02Study technical or fundamental analysis — you can't improvise
  • 03Define a strategy with non-negotiable rules: entry, exit, risk per trade
  • 04Trade in demo mode first until you get consistent results
  • 05Move to real money with capital you can afford to lose
  • 06Record everything, analyze mistakes, optimize over time

The truth without filters

85% of retail traders lose money. Not because trading doesn't work — but because people start without studying, without a plan, with unrealistic expectations. The consistently profitable trader took years. And you won't find them on TikTok showing profits — because they're busy trading.

◈

Recommended minimum capital: $5,000-$10,000. Time before consistent profits: 12-24 months of serious study and practice.

Good for you if...
  • ✓You're analytical, disciplined, handle stress without losing your mind
  • ✓You have capital you don't need to live on
  • ✗NOT for you if you want to earn immediately or can't handle losses emotionally
Chapter 02
DROP-
SHIPPING

What it really is

Selling products online without physically having them. The customer buys from you, you forward the order to the supplier, the supplier ships to the customer. You manage the store and marketing — without ever touching the product. It's not passive. It's not automatic. But it works, if done right.

How the process works

  • 01Choose a niche and reliable suppliers (Zendrop, CJ Dropshipping, European suppliers)
  • 02Build a professional store on Shopify
  • 03Launch ad campaigns on Meta or TikTok to drive traffic
  • 04Test products until you find the winning one
  • 05Optimize ads, price, product page, checkout
  • 06Handle customer service, returns and suppliers

The truth without filters

Dropshipping requires ad budget. Expect to spend $300-$700 on tests before finding a winning product. Anyone who tells you it can be done for free is lying.

◈

Realistic minimum budget to start: $800-$1,500 between store and ads. Don't start with less.

Good for you if...
  • ✓You have starting budget and want a scalable online business
  • ✓You're willing to learn digital marketing on the field
  • ✗NOT for you if you're looking for something passive or have no starting budget
Chapter 03
CRYPTO-
CURRENCY

What it really is

Decentralized digital currencies on blockchain. Bitcoin is the first and most established. Thousands of others exist with very different reliability levels. Critical point: investing in crypto and trading crypto are two completely different activities.

Investing vs Speculating

INVESTING = buying established assets with a long-term thesis (3-5+ years) and holding. DCA strategy: fixed amount every month regardless of price.

SPECULATING = buying and selling short-term exploiting volatility. Very high risk. Most people lose.

How the process works

  • 01Open account on regulated exchange (Coinbase, Kraken)
  • 02Start with small amounts — don't put everything in at once
  • 03Choose strategy: long-term HODl or active trading
  • 04Use hardware wallet for significant amounts
  • 05Manage taxes: capital gains above €2,000 taxed at 26% in Italy
◈

Absolute rule: only invest what you can afford to lose 100%. It's not pessimism — it's the only correct mindset for this market.

Chapter 04
BECOMING
A CHARACTER

What it really is

Building an audience around your person, your skills or your story — and monetizing it. It's not influencer marketing 2015 style. It's professional personal branding in 2025.

How the process works

  • 01Choose a specific, recognizable niche — not generic
  • 02Master one platform before diversifying
  • 03Create content consistently — not when you feel like it, with a plan
  • 04Build real engagement — reply, interact, build community
  • 05Monetize: brand deals, digital products, courses, consulting, membership

The truth without filters

It takes 12-18 months of constant, unpaid work before seeing significant economic results. Most people quit after 6-8 weeks because "it's not growing fast enough".

◈

Don't confuse visibility with income. 100k followers with no strategy = $0. 3k highly engaged followers with a product = great revenue.

Good for you if...
  • ✓You have something specific to say and are consistent by nature
  • ✗NOT for you if you want quick results or can't maintain consistency
Chapter 05
GAMBLING

Yes, we talk about it. Because it exists.

Gambling is a method of earning money. Said that — with all due clarity — let's move on. There's an enormous difference between pure gambling (slots, roulette) and skill-based gambling (poker, value betting). In the first, math is against you by definition. In the second, with study and discipline, you can develop a real statistical edge.

For those with real skill

  • PokerTheory, thousands of hands, session analysis, strict bankroll management
  • ValueStatistical models to find undervalued odds from bookmakers
  • RuleNever risk more than 1-3% of capital on a single bet — whoever breaks this rule loses everything, always

The truth without filters

Pure gambling is a tax on mathematical ignorance. Slots have an RTP of 85-95% — in the long run you always lose, by definition. Professional poker and value betting exist as real income sources — but require years of study and iron emotional management.

◈

Gambling has pathological addiction potential. If you have compulsive tendencies this path is not for you — regardless of skill level.

Chapter 06
MINDSET
In the learning phase
  • →Be humble. You don't know yet. Study those who have actually done the journey.
  • →Don't rush. Haste burns capital and motivation, almost always together.
  • →Accept losses as a learning cost — not as personal failures.
In the operational phase
  • →Be decisive. You have a plan — execute it. Doubt midway is the fastest way to lose.
  • →Separate emotions from decisions. Emotions are information, not instructions.
  • →Stay cold when things go wrong — that's where you see who lasts and who quits.
In the growth phase
  • →Be selective. Not every opportunity deserves your time.
  • →Scale only what already works. Optimize before expanding.
  • →The right people are worth more than any strategy.

How to make a proposal that works

  • 01CLARITY — What you offer, what you ask, what the other gains. No ambiguity.
  • 02VALUE — The benefit for who receives it, not your enthusiasm.
  • 03URGENCY — A real reason to decide now. Not manipulative — concrete.

A proposal that needs to be explained twice is not yet ready to be made.

When to accept and when to say no

Accept if...
  • ✓The risk is calculated on real numbers, not just emotionally
  • ✓The deal is clear, defined, preferably in writing
  • ✓The counterpart has a verifiable track record
Say no if...
  • ✗You feel pressure to decide immediately — always a red flag, no exceptions
  • ✗The numbers don't add up — if you don't understand the model, don't enter
  • ✗The deal is based on unverifiable verbal promises
  • ✗Your gut says no — instinct is the sum of all your past experiences

Capitolo 01
TRADING

Cos'è, davvero

Comprare e vendere asset finanziari — azioni, valute, materie prime, indici — cercando di guadagnare dalla differenza di prezzo nel tempo. Non è magia. Non è fortuna. È un lavoro con regole precise e una curva di apprendimento che dura anni.

Come funziona il processo

  • 01Apri un conto presso un broker regolamentato (Interactive Brokers, Degiro, Saxo)
  • 02Studi analisi tecnica o fondamentale — non si improvvisa
  • 03Definisci una strategia con regole non negoziabili: entrata, uscita, rischio per operazione
  • 04Operi prima in modalità demo fino a risultati costanti
  • 05Passi al reale con capitali che puoi permetterti di perdere
  • 06Registri tutto, analizzi gli errori, ottimizzi nel tempo

La verità senza filtri

L'85% dei trader retail perde soldi. Non perché il trading non funziona — ma perché la gente inizia senza studiare, senza un piano, con aspettative irrealistiche. Il trader profittevole ci ha messo anni. E non lo trovi su TikTok — perché è impegnato a operare.

◈

Capitale minimo consigliato: 5.000-10.000€. Tempo prima di profitti costanti: 12-24 mesi di studio e pratica seria.

Fa per te se...
  • ✓Sei analitico, disciplinato, gestisci lo stress senza perdere la testa
  • ✓Hai capitale che non ti serve per vivere
  • ✗NON fa per te se vuoi guadagnare subito o non sopporti le perdite emotivamente

Capitolo 03
CRIPTO-
VALUTE

Cos'è, davvero

Valute digitali decentralizzate su blockchain. Bitcoin è la prima e più consolidata. Ne esistono migliaia di altre con livelli di affidabilità molto diversi. Punto critico: investire in crypto e fare trading di crypto sono due attività completamente diverse.

Investire vs Speculare

INVESTIRE = comprare asset consolidati con orizzonte lungo (3-5+ anni) e tenerli. Strategia DCA: cifra fissa ogni mese indipendentemente dal prezzo.

SPECULARE = comprare e vendere nel breve sfruttando la volatilità. Rischio molto alto. La maggior parte perde.

Come funziona il processo

  • 01Apri account su exchange regolamentato (Coinbase, Kraken)
  • 02Inizia con cifre piccole — non mettere tutto subito
  • 03Scegli strategia: HODl a lungo o trading attivo
  • 04Usa wallet hardware per importi significativi
  • 05Gestisci le tasse: plusvalenze sopra 2.000€ tassate al 26% in Italia
◈

Regola assoluta: investi solo quello che puoi permetterti di perdere al 100%. Non è pessimismo — è l'unica mentalità corretta per questo mercato.

Capitolo 05
GIOCO
D'AZZARDO

Sì, ne parliamo. Perché esiste.

Il gioco d'azzardo è un metodo di guadagno. Detto questo — con tutta la chiarezza del caso — andiamo avanti. C'è una differenza enorme tra gioco puro (slot, roulette) e gioco con componente di abilità (poker, value betting). Nel primo la matematica è contro di te per definizione. Nel secondo, con studio e disciplina, puoi sviluppare un vantaggio statistico reale.

Per chi ha abilità reale

  • PokerTeoria, migliaia di mani, analisi sessioni, bankroll management rigido
  • ValueModelli statistici per trovare quote sottovalutate dai bookmaker
  • RegolaMai rischiare più dell'1-3% del capitale su una singola puntata — chi viola questa regola perde tutto, sempre

La verità senza filtri

Il gioco puro è una tassa sull'ignoranza matematica. Le slot hanno un RTP dell'85-95% — nel lungo periodo perdi sempre, per definizione. Il poker professionistico e il value betting esistono come reddito reale — ma richiedono anni di studio e gestione emotiva di ferro.

◈

Il gioco d'azzardo ha potenziale di dipendenza patologica. Se hai tendenze compulsive questo percorso non fa per te — indipendentemente dall'abilità.
